Flubby Ghost

Flubby Ghost is an turn based puzzle game with beautifully crafted levels.

An evil witch stole the ghost's powers and scattered them across the island, and now you'll have to help him regain his abilities.

To achieve that you'll have to complete dozens of levels and avoid various enemies.

Platforms: iOS, Android, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X

Release date: 2023
